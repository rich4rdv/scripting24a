One thing you don’t want to miss is the festivals. In summer, we have Thrissur Pooram, takes center stage. It is one of the most spectacular festivals in the country celebrated at the heart of the city, attracting thousands of locals and tourists are involved every year. Thrissur Pooram is India’s grandest temple pageantry, which includes colourful processions of caparisoned elephants and a midnight fireworks display, parasol exchanges, drum concerts that light up the crowded streets of Thrissur.

Athirappilly falls, an 80 ft high waterfall at the threshold of the Sholayar mountain ranges in the Western Ghats is a popular picnic spot. Two amusement water parks, DreamWorld and SilverStorm, are located nearby. Often referred to as the “Niagara Falls” of India, Athirappilly is a favourite shooting location for Indian films.

Most of Thrissur’s attractions involve elephants and fireworks, which some might say it’s a risky combination. But what is adventure without a little risk in it? If you’re an elephant lover, Thrissur will feel like home. One more thing you don’t want to miss is the Helicopter ride offered by the state’s tourism department., which let’s you discover Thrissur’s aerial beauty unveiling the symphony of rural and urban elegance. If you're drawn to the vibrant festivals, natural beauty, or cultural history, Thrissur promises an unforgettable experience. Experience Thrissur with all your senses.
