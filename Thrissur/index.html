About


Thrissur the ‘Cultural Capital of Kerala’, formerly known as Trichur is a district in Kerala State in southern India. Thrissur, a commercial and cultural centre, is the oldest city on the west coast of India. The name Thrissur means “a small sacred place.”, though the city is far from being little. Thrissur is known for its cultural, spiritual, and religious leanings throughout history. Despite modern development, it still tells a lot of stories of its rich past. The most unique feature of Thrissur is its colorful festivals. Even though being a city of many faiths, the whole of Thrissur unites as one when it comes to festivals and celebrations.
