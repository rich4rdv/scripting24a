Thrissur is home to many South Indian entrepreneurs and is a major financial and commercial hub of Kerala. Being one of the major manufacturing centers of gold and ayurvedic products many of its residents are employed in these sectors.

The people of Thrissur take pride in their community and work hard to preserve its history and culture. The city with its temples, old churches and mosques, and a rich heritage, ranks first in the number of domestic tourists visiting Kerala. Thrissur is also one of fastest-developing cities in the state which boasts the highest literacy rate in India. With the affordable health care services, Thrissur is considered one of the best cities for peaceful living. Also, it is a safe destination for tourists.
